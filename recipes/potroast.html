Pot Roast

Recipe

This is a delicious recipe I found online. It's not homemade or anything.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Vegetables
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Flour
  • Wine

Steps

  1. Let beef rest uncovered in the fridge, salted for a day
  2. Cook beef in a pot roast with other ingredients and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours

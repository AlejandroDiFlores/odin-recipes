Sopita

Recipe

This is a delicious recipe I found online. It's not homemade or anything.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Bouillon
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Roast pasta in oil until slightly browned, then add onions
  2. Add tomato sauce, one block of bouillon and water
  3. Add seasonings to your desired taste and cook until pasta is al dente

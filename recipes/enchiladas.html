Enchiladas

Recipe

This is a delicious recipe I found online. It's not homemade or anything.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cheese
  • Onion
  • Beans
  • Tortillas
  • Chiles

Steps

  1. Cook chicken
  2. Shred cheese, cut onions, heat beans
  3. Add ingredients to the tortilla, roll them up and cover in sauce
